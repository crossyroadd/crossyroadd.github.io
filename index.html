Crossy Road Unblocked

Crossy Road game is a cute chicken's challenging struggle on the roads, you should carefully control the chicken while crossing the road in heavy traffic. The orange jumping chicken is lost and wandering on the roads. It is trying to cross the roads in heavy traffic. This situation is not easy at all, it is even in danger of being crushed. Complete the tasks by moving quickly and carefully on these roads where cars pass quickly. You should help the chicken trying to cross the road for its most difficult task. You should use the woods as steps to jump over the water. A fun game where you can show off your skill is waiting for you.

If you step backwards, your score decreases this time. The race horse wants to go to the horse ranch by passing through heavy traffic, railways and rivers. You need to evaluate the situations when the road is empty without hitting the cars. You should also pass over the rails when the train is not coming. Play the crossy road game by jumping over the logs floating in the river while crossing the river.

How to Play the Crossy Road Game?

Played with Arrow Keys and SPACE key.
